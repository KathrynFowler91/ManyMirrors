Useful links

Find below links to useful faith and LGBT+ resources. If we've missed any or you would like to see others included please get in contact and let us know.

  • Superbia

    www.superbia.org.uk

    Superbia provides a year-round calendar of LGBT cultural events in Manchester. The page works alongside Manchester Pride and celebrates LGBT life across the city by funding and advertising a wide variety of projects.

  • Manchester Pride

    www.manchesterpride.com

    Manchester Pride is a registered charity that campaigns for equality and challenges discrimination; creates opportunity for engagement and participation and celebrates lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (LGBT) life.

  • LGBT Foundation

    lgbt.foundation/

    LGBT Foundation is a national charity delivering advice, support and information services to lesbian, gay, bisexual and trans (LGBT) communities.

  • The Proud Trust

    www.theproudtrust.org

    The Proud Trust is a life saving and life enhancing organisation that helps young people empower themselves to make a positive change for themselves and their communities. We do this through youth groups, peer support, managing the LGBT centre for Manchester, delivering of training and events, Campaigns, undertaking research and creating resources.

  • First Wednesday

    firstwednesday.lgbt/

    'First Wednesday' is a support and social group for lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (LGBT) people in the Greater Manchester area who are in the asylum process.

  • Rainbow Noir

    www.facebook.com/rainbownoirmcr

    Rainbow Noir is a social and peer support group for people of colour who identify as LGBTQI

  • Open Church Network

    openchurch.network/theme/lgbt-inclusion

    Conversation, dialogue and resources around how the Church and local churches can better include and embrace people who are lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender. We will also hear people's stories and guide those who need support to appropriate places.

  • Open Table

    opentable.lgbt/

    Open Table is an ecumenical Christian worship community which offers a warm welcome to people who are: Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer / Questioning, Intersex, Asexual (LGBTQIA) and all who seek an inclusive Church.

  • Christians at Pride

    www.christiansatpride.com/

    Christians at Pride exists to provide a joyful, loving, inclusive Christian presence at Pride festivals in London and elsewhere in the UK. Whether you're young or old, catholic or evangelical, lesbian, gay, bi, trans, asexual, or straight, you'd be so welcome to come and join us. Supportive friends, allies, family members and church leaders all very welcome too.

  • Metropolitan Church, Manchester

    metropolitanchurch.org.uk

    A Christ-centred congregation which serves, celebrates and affirms the lesbian, gay, bisexual and transgender (lgbt) communities of Manchester and North West England.
