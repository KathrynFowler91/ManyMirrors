How to get involved

Everyone is welcome to join our LGBT interfaith group. There are a wide variety of ways in which you can get involved to our community and all contributions, big or small, are welcomed. Below is a short list all the opportunites we have for you to get involved.

  • Attending Many Mirror social events. Find out more from the events section of the website.
  • Connect us with your LGBT or faith group, we would love to hear about the work that you do and maybe collaborate on some events. Feel free to drop us an email or send us a message through the contact section of the website.
  • If you would like to offer any expertise towards the organisation of Many Mirrors events or socials we would love to hear your ideas.
  • We would love to hear any ideas for future news posts, whether it is a book review, an event you have attended or a personal experience you would like to share please get in touch.
  • Join the Facebook page and follow us on Twitter to keep up-to-date on any events.
